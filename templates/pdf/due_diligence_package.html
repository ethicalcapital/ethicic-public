Risk Management Framework

Investment Risk Controls

  • Portfolio Construction: Systematic diversification across sectors, geographies, and market capitalizations
  • Position Limits: Maximum position sizes to prevent concentration risk
  • ESG Risk Assessment: Evaluation of ESG-related risks in security selection and portfolio construction
  • Liquidity Management: Regular assessment of portfolio liquidity and cash flow requirements
  • Performance Monitoring: Daily portfolio monitoring and monthly performance attribution

Operational Risk Management

  • Compliance Oversight: Regular compliance reviews and regulatory filing management
  • Cybersecurity: Multi-layered security protocols and regular security assessments
  • Business Continuity: Comprehensive disaster recovery and business continuity planning
  • Third-Party Risk: Due diligence and ongoing monitoring of key service providers
