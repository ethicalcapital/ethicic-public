Investment Universe

This strategy invests in companies that pass our comprehensive ethical screening process, focusing on businesses that create positive societal impact while delivering strong financial returns.

Portfolio Construction

We maintain a concentrated portfolio of our highest-conviction ideas, typically holding 15-25 positions with target allocation of approximately 4% per holding. This approach allows us to maintain meaningful exposure to our best ideas while ensuring adequate diversification.

Sector Allocation

Our sector allocation is driven by bottom-up security selection rather than top-down sector targets. We maintain broad diversification across sectors while avoiding industries that conflict with our ethical criteria.
