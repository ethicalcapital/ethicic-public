PRINCIPLES THAT GUIDE OUR WORK

Humility is irreplaceable

Hubris hurts clients, and has no place here. We acknowledge what we don't know and continuously learn.

Investing isn't speculation

If we can't tell what's what, it's a problem. We invest in real businesses with clear value propositions.

Costs kill compounding

Avoiding expenses is the easiest way to create value. We keep our fees low and our process efficient.

Simplicity is sophisticated

Complex frameworks are for architects, not investors. Clear thinking leads to better decisions.

Collaboration drives creativity

Our network is full of novel perspectives. Every client conversation makes us smarter.

Many things can be true at once

The market operates with more than one mindset. We embrace nuance and avoid false binaries.

Change is slow, then sudden

Innovation needs space and time to mature. We're patient with our investments and our process.

Progress over perfection
