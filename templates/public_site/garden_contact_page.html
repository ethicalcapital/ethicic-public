GARDEN PLATFORM

Open Source Research & Portfolio Management

The platform powering ethical investing research and portfolio management at Ethical Capital

Garden Platform is our open-source research and portfolio management system, built to support sophisticated investment research, compliance monitoring, and client relationship management. Originally developed for our own use, we're exploring ways to make it available as a hosted service.

PLATFORM CAPABILITIES

Garden Platform combines research workflows, portfolio management, compliance monitoring, and client communication in a unified, extensible system.

RESEARCH WORKFLOWS

Structured research processes, entity extraction, knowledge graph construction, and AI-powered analysis tools.

PORTFOLIO MANAGEMENT

Complete portfolio construction, risk analysis, performance attribution, and rebalancing automation.

COMPLIANCE MONITORING

Automated compliance checks, regulatory reporting, and audit trail management for investment advisers.

CLIENT COMMUNICATIONS

Integrated CRM, automated reporting, and client portal for transparent investment management.

TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE

Built with modern web technologies and designed for scalability, extensibility, and compliance.

  • Django Backend — Full-featured web framework with PostgreSQL database and Redis caching
  • AI Integration — Multi-provider AI system with OpenAI, Anthropic, and open-source model support
  • Real-time Updates — WebSocket connections for live portfolio updates and research collaboration
  • Extensible Design — Modular architecture supporting custom workflows and integrations
  • Compliance Ready — Built-in audit logging, data retention, and regulatory reporting capabilities
  • Open Source — Available on GitHub with active development and community contributions
